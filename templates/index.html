Our platform specializes in converting Ontology Modeling Language (OML) files into Bayesian networks, unlocking a world of possibilities for data analysis and decision-making.

What is an OML File?

OML (Ontology Modeling Language) files are structured representations of knowledge within a specific domain. They define the relationships between different concepts, entities, and their attributes, making them crucial for semantic data processing and ontology engineering.

What is a Bayesian Network?

A Bayesian network is a graphical model that represents a set of variables and their conditional dependencies via a directed acyclic graph (DAG). It is used for probabilistic reasoning, enabling the computation of probabilities for various outcomes, given certain evidence.

From Structure to Insight

Our web application seamlessly converts your OML files into Bayesian networks. This process not only visualizes the intricate relationships and dependencies encoded in your OML file but also enables the application of Bayesian inference to derive probabilities of interest based on given evidence.

Empower Your Decision-Making

  • Predictive Analysis: Forecast outcomes based on your current data.
  • Diagnostic Reasoning: Identify probable causes for observed events.
  • Decision Support: Evaluate the effects of potential decisions before taking action.
